1. Game's principle

Valorant is a FPS game with 2 teams of 5 players, each team has several roles such as duellist, initiator, controller etc. The goal of each game is that the first team to win 13 rounds wins the game while defending and attacking. In addition, you must play with the "spike" a time bomb that must be ignited or de-ignited depending on the time of the game, that is to say, attacker or defender.
